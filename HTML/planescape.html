Planescape: Torment - Planes Description

Planescape: Torment is a role-playing video game set in the Planescape universe, a unique and philosophical fantasy setting. The game takes players on a captivating journey through various planes of existence, each with its distinct characteristics and inhabitants.

Sigil, the City of Doors

Sigil serves as the central hub of the multiverse, a sprawling city located at the center of all planes. It is a cosmopolitan and mysterious place, inhabited by beings from countless worlds and dimensions. Sigil is known for its multitude of portals, allowing travel to other planes and realms.

The Outlands

The Outlands is a plane that connects all other planes together. It is a vast, flat, and desolate landscape featuring giant spires known as the Spire of the Outlands. It serves as a neutral ground and a crossroads for travelers journeying between different realms.

Baator, the Nine Hells

Baator is a lawful evil plane divided into nine layers, each ruled by a powerful archdevil. It is a plane of torment and punishment, where souls are sent to suffer eternally for their sins. The Nine Hells is known for its infernal bureaucracy and intricate political schemes.

The Abyss

The Abyss is a chaotic and infinite plane of pure evil. It is home to countless demonic beings and serves as their breeding ground. The Abyss consists of an endless number of layers, each more twisted and nightmarish than the last.

Mechanus, the Clockwork Nirvana of Order

Mechanus is a plane of perfect order, governed by a strict hierarchy and precise laws. It is a realm of clockwork machinery and mathematical precision, with modrons, mechanical beings, serving as its inhabitants. The plane operates on a precise schedule and adherence to rules.

Limbo, the Plane of Chaos

Limbo is an ever-changing and chaotic plane, where reality is constantly in flux. It is a realm of swirling energies and shifting landscapes, devoid of any stable structure. Limbo is home to creatures embodying chaos and creativity, with bizarre and unpredictable behavior.

These are just a few examples of the planes within the Planescape universe as portrayed in Planescape: Torment. Each plane offers unique experiences, themes, and challenges, contributing to the game's rich and immersive world.
